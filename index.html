About Indonesia

Indonesia, officially the Republic of Indonesia, is a country in Southeast Asia and Oceania, between the Indian and Pacific oceans. It consists of more than seventeen thousand islands, including Sumatra, Java, Sulawesi, and parts of Borneo and New Guinea.

Indonesia is the world's largest island country and the 14th largest country by land area, at 1,904,569 square kilometers. With over 270 million people, Indonesia is the world's fourth-most populous country and the most populous Muslim-majority country.

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Jakarta
  • Official Language: Indonesian
  • Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)
  • National Motto: "Bhinneka Tunggal Ika" (Unity in Diversity)
  • National Anthem: "Indonesia Raya"
